# Governance Replay Policy

## Objective
Enable replay-capable governance evidence validation.

## Replay Scope
Replay artifacts may include:
- governance events
- escalation traces
- export operations
- checksum verification
- runtime telemetry
- vendor review evidence

## Replay Requirements
- append-only evidence preservation
- timestamp continuity
- checksum validation
- immutable export references
- reproducible governance state

## Governance Assurance Goals
- audit reproducibility
- evidence continuity
- operational verification
- deployment resilience

Core Principle:
Time turns behavior into infrastructure.

Data Axiom:
Behavior is the most honest data there is.
